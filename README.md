# ArtistPortfolio
This is An artist webpage to schedule tour-dates, shops, and albums to the public!

1) First comes the smooth, and fully functional HOME PAGE with unique and aesthetic font choices, we are able to grab the viewers attention right form the start! With its dynamic background visual, viewers or fans can joyfully see the tour dates of their favourite artists and buy tickets. Better yet, with the functional links at the footer of the webpage, the viewer can connect with the artist's social media with the touch of a click!!

2) Secondly comes the store page, where fans can easily add their fav merch or item to their cart to later purchase it!
